Volunteer Mentor at Coursera

About this Project

When I was 12 years old, I enrolled into Coursera's Code Yourself! An Intoduction to Programming Course to enhance my programming knowledge. After weeks since I finished the course, I was invited by Coursera to volunteer as a mentor to the course (I have to wait for months before accepting as I'm not sure if they would accept a 12 year old girl). I have been a mentor fo 3 years, and my job is to answer student questions, filter the discussion forums, and sometimes check student work.
